Content

Home page

The Why We live in a day and age where people donâ€™t know where to turn for real information. This site will be a big step in the right direction to help remedy that. The main focus of this site will be on political and social issues with the approach and content presented in a more unique way. Sections The topics will be compromised of three sections mainly. One section will strictly be all available and reliable data on any given subject while strictly being presented objectively. This section will continue to be updated The 2 other sections will be opinion sections from both the politically left and right. This will comprise of those considered more mainstream of their particular party which would hopefully give a clear perspective from each party. Media, including debates, will also be included. An additional section with popular opinions from other parties may be considered in the future as well. Benefits This site will be immensely helpful by allowing people to escape their echo chambers, be more informed, and be able to make up their own minds on these subjects. It will help people stay up to date on information without any of it being suppressed. Future As the site grows, it will continue to offer more options, including entertainment.
